Jeffrey and Bandit

Jeffrey and Bandit were born in captivity at the Danville Fair. It was only through a courageous resuce attempt that they were able to be placed into a good loving home.

  • Scientific Name: CuteiesMaximus
  • Average Length: 12"
  • Average Lifespan: Infinity and Beyond
  • Habitat: The hearts and minds of children everywhere.

Jeffery and Bandit are the cutest animals in the known universe. It is rumored that long ago in a time before time, that they were the rulers of all the lands. They ruled with compassion, mercy and love unlike anyone had ever ruled before. It was not until the evil lord Drakmath conjured up a wicked spell and trapped them in these super cute and adorable bodies. Now they live out their days providing precious love and adoration to all who gaze upon them.
